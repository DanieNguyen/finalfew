Sideline

For my first Dribble post, I was inspired to wholly focus on what I wanted to visually create. This was not a UX project, but a graphic design exercise for myself (unless you want to count me as a reflexive user) to practice new aesthetic trends.

I chose to design a Sports Mobile App screen highlighting LeBron James, my favorite player. In this screen, I experimented mainly with borders and textual hierarchy.


This Website

Although I have used Wix, HTML Templates, and other website creation tools before, I wanted to challenge myself this summer to design, code, and implement my own website. Even though I am terrible at coding, it is always fun solving how to make everything formatted and user-friendly.

Mele

To explore logo designs, I have been coming up with random mobile apps that I think would be useful. Mele is a music streaming service similar to Pandora. However, the app would use computer learning/AI to allow users to customize stations through options like tempo, instruments played, language, and genre.
