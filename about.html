tôi - me.

My name is Daniel, and I am an aspiring designer currently studying my final year of Human Centered Design and Engineering at the University of Washington.

I grew up mainly in Washington, born and raised in the Snohomish Country area except for a couple years in elementary school where I lived in Boynton Beach, FL.

I started appreciating design when I discovered left handed scissors and composition notebooks in middle school. I hope to continue evolving as a designer disregarding career labels such as ux, graphic, visual, product, or interaction. My goal as a designer is to create great experiences for people, through a variety of mediums, carefully considering sociocultural, economic, environmental and other factors.

When I am not being a designer, I like to read fantasy novels, play basketball, go bouldering, take my dog Noodle to the beach, eat out with my siblings, read manga, play video games, and watch sports.

Thank you for reading about me!
